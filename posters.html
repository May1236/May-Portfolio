Poster Collection · Illustration · Adobe Illustrator

Nature, in three frames.

A cohesive collection of three nature-inspired posters — mountains, ocean, sun and sky — drawn from Vietnamese landscapes and built to evoke freedom, calm and limitless exploration.

IllustrationColour SystemComposition Series DesignAdobe Illustrator

Role

Concept, design & illustration

Tool

Adobe Illustrator

Scope

3-poster cohesive series

Constraint

~8 hours, one palette

Overview

One palette, one feeling.

This collection captures the beauty of mountains, the vastness of the ocean and the endless sky. Each poster highlights a different natural element, yet all three share a consistent colour palette and artistic language — so they read instantly as a family while each still tells its own story of freedom and exploration.

The goal

A series that holds together.

The brief: complete three nature-inspired posters featuring mountains, the ocean, the sun and the sky — with a single consistent palette and artistic style — within roughly eight hours in Adobe Illustrator. The real challenge wasn't drawing one nice poster; it was making three feel unmistakably like one collection.

Creative vision

Nature meets human presence.

I explored the harmony between nature and human presence through bold, abstract landscapes. Layered shapes create depth and movement; a warm-yet-contrasting palette evokes nostalgia and serenity. Deep blues bring stability, warm oranges and reds add energy, and soft gradients keep everything flowing — anchored by my own Midnight Blue & Amber for contrast and warmth.

Rationale

Why these choices.

The layered composition builds depth and movement, reinforcing the vastness of nature. The warm-yet-contrasting palette carries the emotion — deep blues for stability, warm tones for energy. The lone figure in a boat in the third piece adds solitude and exploration, tying human presence to the landscape. Together the three balance simplicity with storytelling: engaging at a glance, thoughtful on a second look.

Reflection

Freedom, by design.

This collection aligns closely with who I am — a deep love of nature and a desire for freedom, expressed through designs that feel unbound. I want the work to evoke relaxation, harmony with nature, and the freedom to do what you love, away from everyday noise.
